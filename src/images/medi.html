Results





The competitions are conflicting. The concepts of mutual co-existance are breaking. To address the issue, International society of Self Improvement, Meditation and Complete health was established for achieving synergy, synchrony and self-improvement among the human beings round the globe.

The term ISICH is the short form of International Society of Self Improvement, Meditation and Complete health- where - meditation-M is silent. It also sounds like the word- ISHQ- ISK -ISICH means LOVE. It is by, of, for the love with oneself and every human being. Thus it aspires to bring together- innumerable worldwide loving-kind likeminded people for the above noble objective who could develop the scientific doctrines of mutual love, improvement among the humanity. Such few steps can turn into countless steps to create innovative ways and direction to humanity.



Mission Statement





• ISICH follows a holistic approach towards achieving best result for individual health goals, scientifically proven meditative and physical exercises, physiotherapy and diet counseling under the guidance of our team of expert doctors, skilled therapists and technicians.

• Aspire for diseases and stress free society by inducting people to simple ISICH Cardio-metabolic and holistic - Fitness program which keep them away from stress, depression and anxiety.

• In the era of stressful life all over the word, our organization aim to build individuals with positive attitude towards life through the doctrine of meditation, self improvement and complete health so as to be strong, bold and undisturbed in midst of the adversities of life.

• To make awareness of concept of Pro-health for control of life style diseases like Blood Pressure, Diabetes, Obesity, Stress, Depression and Insomnia etc.

• To live with integrity and to make a difference in the lives of oneself and others irrespective of artificial barrier of castes. creed, religions and nations and to work for women empowerment and upliftment of weaker sections of society



Aims and Objectives





•To achieve above mission.

•To build a society in the world whose constituents give enough importance even to little which brings synergy, synchrony and self improvement among individuals, families.
